    • The Origin Story of Treasure Island

    The settlers who arrived in Florida bought the land, now know as Treasure Island. As a way to promote the island, they did something theatrical. According to the legend of the island, the owner buried a chest filled with gold, and later, he "discovered" it. People flocked to the island in search of treasure.
