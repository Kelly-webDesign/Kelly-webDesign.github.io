SPECTACULAR SEASONS

By Kelly Walsh


Packed with trips to the beach

Swimming is the skill that I teach

Anticipation for the right wave

Exploring a local cave

Kayaking in the lake

Fear of running into a snake

Clean my dog’s dirty paw

Play a game of baseball

Wear a jacket I’m told

Hope I don’t get too cold

Teachers I have to please

Wishing it was 90 degrees

Hate the snow on the ground

No time to play around
